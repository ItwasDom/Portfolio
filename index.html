About Me

I’m Dominic Martinez, a passionate photographer and cinematographer based in San Antonio, TX. With a background in Digital Cinematography from Full Sail University and years of experience in both photography and video production, I bring stories to life through my lens.

My work is dedicated to capturing moments that resonate emotionally while blending artistic vision with technical precision. Whether it’s a cinematic narrative or a single, striking image, I strive to create visuals that speak volumes.
